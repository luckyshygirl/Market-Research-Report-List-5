<p><p>Commercial LED strip light market consists of two main types: Flexible LED strip light and Hard LED strip light. Flexible LED strip lights are thin and bendable, making them ideal for curved or irregular surfaces. They are often used for decorative lighting in commercial spaces. On the other hand, Hard LED strip lights are rigid and provide a strong, even light output. They are commonly used for task lighting in commercial settings where a more concentrated light source is needed. Both types offer energy efficiency and long lifespan, making them popular choices for businesses.</p></p>
