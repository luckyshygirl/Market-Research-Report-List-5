<p><p>Liquid Epoxy Resins are divided into two main types based on viscosity: Low viscosity and High viscosity. Low viscosity Liquid Epoxy Resins are characterized by their thin consistency and excellent flow properties, making them ideal for applications requiring easy mixing and good wetting properties. On the other hand, High viscosity Liquid Epoxy Resins are thicker in texture, offering better gap filling and sag resistance, making them suitable for applications where high build and vertical stability are important.</p></p>
