<p><p>Diamond Suspension market can be divided into two main types - Oil Based and Water Based. Oil based diamond suspensions use oil as a carrier medium for the diamond particles, providing good lubrication and heat dissipation. Water based diamond suspensions, on the other hand, use water as a carrier medium, making them environmentally friendly and easier to clean up. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications based on the desired cutting or polishing results.</p></p>
