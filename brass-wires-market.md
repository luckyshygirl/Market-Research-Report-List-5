<p><p>Brass wires market is divided into different types based on their composition and properties. Alpha Brass wires contain only zinc as an alloying element and are known for their excellent cold working capabilities. Alpha-beta Brass wires have a combination of alpha and beta phases, offering a balance of strength and ductility. Beta Brass wires contain high copper content, providing excellent corrosion resistance. Gamma Brass wires have a high zinc content, making them suitable for decorative applications. White Brass wires contain a combination of copper, zinc, and nickel, offering a silvery appearance.</p></p>
