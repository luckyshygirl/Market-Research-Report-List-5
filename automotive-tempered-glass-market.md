<p><p>Full tempered glass is a type of automotive tempered glass that undergoes a specialized heating and cooling process to increase its strength and resistance to impacts. This type of glass is used for windshields, windows, and other automotive applications where safety and durability are crucial. On the other hand, zone tempered glass is a variation of tempered glass where only specific areas of the glass are tempered, leaving other areas untreated. This allows for customized strength and flexibility in different parts of the glass panel.</p></p>
