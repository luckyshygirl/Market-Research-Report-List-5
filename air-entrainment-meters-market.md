<p><p>Air entrainment meters are devices used to measure the air content in fresh concrete. There are two main types in the market: water column type air entrainment meters and pressure gauge type air entrainment meters. </p><p>Water column type meters measure air content by the displacement of water in a graduated column, while pressure gauge type meters measure air content by the pressure difference caused by air bubbles in the concrete. Both types are essential tools for ensuring the quality and durability of concrete structures.</p></p>
