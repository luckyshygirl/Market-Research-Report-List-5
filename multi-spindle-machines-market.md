<p><p>Multi spindle machines come in two main types in the market: adjustable and fixed. Adjustable multi spindle machines allow for individual spindles to be adjusted independently, offering versatility in machining different parts with varying specifications. On the other hand, fixed multi spindle machines have a set configuration of spindles, limiting flexibility but often offering higher production speeds and efficiency for machining parts with consistent specifications. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
