<p><p>Cardiac ablation technology market includes various types such as Radiofrequency, Cryoablation, Laser, Ultrasound, Microwave, and others. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat to destroy abnormal heart tissue, while Cryoablation utilizes extreme cold. Laser ablation uses high-energy light to target specific tissue, while Ultrasound uses sound waves for ablation. Microwave ablation heats tissue using microwaves. Other types of ablation technologies may include electroporation or high-intensity focused ultrasound. These technologies offer minimally invasive options for treating various cardiac conditions.</p></p>
