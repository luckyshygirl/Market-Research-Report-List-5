<p><p>Air-to-Air Refueling Market consists of different types such as MPRS or Multi-point Refueling System, which enables multiple aircraft to be refueled simultaneously. Wing-to-Wing Refueling involves transferring fuel between two aircraft flying in close formation. Simple Grappling Refueling uses a probe on the receiving aircraft to connect with a drogue on the tanker aircraft for fuel transfer. All these methods play a vital role in extending the range and endurance of military aircraft during long-distance missions.</p></p>
