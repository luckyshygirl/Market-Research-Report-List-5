<p><p>ADAS Domain Controllers can be categorized into three main types: Domain Controller below L2, L2-L3 Domain Controller, and Domain Controller above L3. The Domain Controller below L2 mainly focuses on simple driver-assist functions such as lane-keeping assistance. The L2-L3 Domain Controller combines features like adaptive cruise control and automated lane changing. The Domain Controller above L3 offers advanced autonomous driving capabilities including fully autonomous driving without driver intervention in certain conditions. These different types cater to varying levels of automation in vehicles.</p></p>
