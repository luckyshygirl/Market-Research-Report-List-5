<p><p>Indoor Positioning Systems (IPS) utilize various market types to determine the location of objects or people indoors. Radio Waves IPS use wireless signals to triangulate positions, Magnetic Fields IPS detect changes in magnetic fields to track movement, and Acoustic Signals IPS analyze sound waves for positioning. There are also other market types such as Infrared and Visible Light IPS that use light-based technology to locate objects. Each IPS market type offers unique advantages and applications for indoor positioning solutions.</p></p>
