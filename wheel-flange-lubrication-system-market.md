<p><ul><li>SolidStickSystem</li><li>SpraySystem</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wheel Flange Lubrication System market offers two main types of systems: Solid Stick System and Spray System. The Solid Stick System involves a solid lubricant stick that is manually inserted into the system to provide consistent lubrication to wheel flanges. On the other hand, the Spray System consists of a mechanism that automatically sprays lubricant onto the wheel flanges as the train moves along the track. Both systems aim to reduce friction, wear, and noise, ultimately improving the efficiency and lifespan of trains.</p></p>
