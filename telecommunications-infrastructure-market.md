<p><p>Telecommunications infrastructure market types include lattice towers, guyed towers, monopole towers, stealth towers, and others. Lattice towers are lattice-shaped structures used for transmitting signals over long distances. Guyed towers are supported by guy wires connected to the ground. Monopole towers are single, slender structures commonly used in urban areas. Stealth towers are designed to blend in with surrounding environments. Other types of towers may include self-supporting towers or rooftop structures. Each type of tower serves specific purposes in the telecommunications industry.</p></p>
