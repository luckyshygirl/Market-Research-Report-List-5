<p><ul><li>Inertial Seismometer</li><li>Extensometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seismic sensor market is divided into two main types: inertial seismometers and extensometers. Inertial seismometers measure ground motion based on the principles of inertia, while extensometers detect strain in the earth's crust. Inertial seismometers are commonly used in earthquake monitoring and structural health monitoring, while extensometers are used for measuring deformation in civil infrastructure and natural geological formations. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in detecting and monitoring seismic activity for various applications.</p></p>
