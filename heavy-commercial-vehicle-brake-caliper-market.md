<p><p>The Heavy Commercial Vehicle Brake Caliper market consists of two main types: Single Piston Caliper and Multi-Piston Caliper. Single Piston Calipers have a single piston that applies pressure to the brake pads, while Multi-Piston Calipers have multiple pistons that distribute pressure evenly for improved braking performance. Single Piston Calipers are commonly found in smaller vehicles, while Multi-Piston Calipers are preferred for heavy commercial vehicles due to their ability to provide greater stopping power and control.</p></p>
