<p><p>The Automotive Sealed Lead-acid Battery Market consists of two main types - AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) Battery and Gel Battery. AGM batteries use a glass mat to absorb the electrolyte, providing a maintenance-free and leak-proof design suitable for start-stop systems. On the other hand, Gel batteries use a silica-based gel electrolyte, offering better resistance to vibration and deep discharges. Both types are commonly used in vehicles for various applications, providing reliable power for starting, lighting, and ignition systems.</p></p>
