<p><p>Steel abrasives are commonly used in various industries for surface preparation, cleaning, and finishing processes. The two main types of steel abrasives are steel shot and steel grit. Steel shot is spherical in shape and is used for smooth surface finishing, while steel grit has angular particles and is preferred for more aggressive cleaning and blasting applications. Both types of steel abrasives are durable, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly options for metal surface treatment.</p></p>
