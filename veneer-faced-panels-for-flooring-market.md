<p><ul><li>Particleboard Faced</li><li>MDF Faced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Veneer-faced panels for flooring come in two main types: particleboard faced and MDF faced. Particleboard faced panels are made by bonding a thin layer of real wood veneer to a particleboard core. They are cost-effective and provide a natural wood look. MDF faced panels, on the other hand, use a medium-density fiberboard core with a veneer layer. These panels offer a smoother surface and better edge finishing compared to particleboard faced panels. Both types are popular choices for flooring due to their durability and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
