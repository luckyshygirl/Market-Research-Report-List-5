<p><p>Fixed displacement measuring pumps have a set flow rate that does not change, making them ideal for applications where a consistent amount of fluid needs to be dispensed. Variable displacement measuring pumps, on the other hand, allow for the adjustment of the flow rate based on the specific requirements of the application. This flexibility makes them suitable for situations where the amount of fluid being dispensed may need to vary. The market for these pumps caters to industries that require precision in fluid dispensing processes.</p></p>
