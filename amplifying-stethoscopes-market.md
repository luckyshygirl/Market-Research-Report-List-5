<p><p>Amplifying stethoscopes are available in two main types - Single-Head Stethoscope and Dual-Head Stethoscope. Single-Head stethoscopes have a single chest piece that is used for listening to the heart, lungs, and other bodily sounds. Dual-Head stethoscopes have two chest pieces, usually with a diaphragm and a bell, allowing for more versatility in listening to different frequencies of sounds. Both types of stethoscopes are commonly used by healthcare professionals for auscultation and diagnosis of patients.</p></p>
