<p><strong>The Boat Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Bearing</li><li>Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat bearings market is segmented into two main types - rolling bearings and sliding bearings. Rolling bearings utilize balls or rollers to reduce friction and support loads, offering high efficiency and durability. These are widely used in boat engines and propellers. On the other hand, sliding bearings rely on a sliding motion to reduce friction and support loads. They are commonly used in boat propeller shafts and rudder assemblies. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth operation and performance of boat components.</p></p>
