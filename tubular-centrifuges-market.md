<p><p>Tubular centrifuges are commonly categorized into two main types - GF Type and GQ Type. GF Type centrifuges are primarily used for solid-liquid separation in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemical processing. On the other hand, GQ Type centrifuges are designed for liquid-liquid separation applications in industries like oil and gas, biodiesel, and waste water treatment. Both types of centrifuges offer efficient and precise separation of different substances based on their specific gravity and particle size.</p></p>
