<p><p>Melamine-faced panels are a popular choice in construction for their versatility and durability. There are two main types of melamine-faced panels in the market: MF-C (melamine-faced chipboard) and MF-MDF (melamine-faced medium-density fiberboard). MF-C panels are made of wood chips bonded together with resin and covered with a melamine coating, while MF-MDF panels are made of refined wood fibers compressed together and coated with melamine. Both types of panels provide a cost-effective and stylish solution for interior and furniture applications in construction projects.</p></p>
