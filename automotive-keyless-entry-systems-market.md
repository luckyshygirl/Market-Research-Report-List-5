<p><strong>The Automotive Keyless Entry Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radio Frequency Identification (RFID)</li><li>Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Keyless Entry Systems come in two main types: Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). RFID systems allow for contactless access to vehicles by sending signals from a key fob to a receiver in the car, unlocking doors and starting the engine. BLE systems use Bluetooth technology to establish a connection between the key fob and the car, enabling seamless access without physical contact. Both systems provide convenient and secure ways to enter and operate vehicles.</p></p>
