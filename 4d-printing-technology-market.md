<p><p>4D Printing Technology Market types include Inkjet Printing, Melt Deposition Modeling (FDM), Direct Ink Writing (DIW), Stereolithography (SLA), Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). Inkjet Printing involves materials being deposited layer by layer using inkjet technology. FDM creates objects by extruding thermoplastic material layer upon layer. DIW utilizes a robotic arm to directly extrude material into desired shapes. SLA uses a UV laser to solidify liquid resin into a solid object. EBM and SLM use high-powered lasers to melt and fuse metal powders into solid shapes.</p></p>
