<p><ul><li>Downhole Equipment</li><li>Surface Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Well testing equipment market can be classified into two main types: Downhole Equipment and Surface Equipment. Downhole Equipment includes tools and instruments used for measuring pressure, temperature, and flow rates downhole in the wellbore. Surface Equipment, on the other hand, includes equipment such as separators, flow meters, and choke valves that are used at the surface to control and monitor the flow of fluids from the well. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient and accurate testing of wells.</p></p>
