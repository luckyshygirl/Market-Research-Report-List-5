<p><p>Flame resistant plastic materials are classified into different types based on their fire retardant properties. HB resistant plastics are self-extinguishing but do not have specific flame ratings. VO resistant plastics have a vertical burn rating and are considered self-extinguishing. V1, 5VB, and 5VA resistant plastics have higher flame retardant properties, with 5VA being the most resistant to flames. These classifications help in choosing the most suitable flame resistant plastic for specific applications where fire safety is a priority.</p></p>
