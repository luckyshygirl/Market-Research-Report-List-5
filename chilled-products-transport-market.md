<p><strong>The Chilled Products Transport Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vapor Compression Systems</li><li>Cryogenic Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chilled products transport market offers various systems for maintaining low temperatures during transportation. Vapor compression systems utilize a compressor to cool refrigerant gas, which circulates throughout the storage area, while cryogenic systems use liquid nitrogen or carbon dioxide for rapid cooling. Vapor compression systems are more common and cost-effective, while cryogenic systems offer faster cooling but may be more expensive to operate. Both systems play a vital role in ensuring the freshness and safety of chilled products during transportation.</p></p>
