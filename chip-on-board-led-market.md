<p><p>Chip-on-Board LED (COB LED) is a type of LED packaging technology where multiple LED chips are mounted directly onto a substrate to form a single module. The automotive industry is a major driver of the market, with COB LEDs being used in headlights, interior lighting, and displays. Additionally, the growing adoption of COB LEDs in residential, commercial, and industrial lighting applications is fueling market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Chip-on-Board LED market include the development of high-power COB LEDs for outdoor lighting applications, the integration of smart lighting features, and the use of COB LEDs in horticultural lighting for indoor agriculture.
