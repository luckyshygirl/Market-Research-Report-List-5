<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Airbag Inflator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrotechnic Inflator</li><li>Stored Gas Inflator</li><li>Hybrid Inflator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Airbag Inflator Market is segmented into three types: Pyrotechnic Inflator, Stored Gas Inflator, and Hybrid Inflator. Pyrotechnic inflators use a chemical reaction to generate gas for inflating the airbag. Stored Gas inflators store compressed gas in a cylinder until deployment. Hybrid inflators combine aspects of both pyrotechnic and stored gas inflators for a controlled and efficient deployment of the airbag. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different commercial vehicle applications.</p></p>
