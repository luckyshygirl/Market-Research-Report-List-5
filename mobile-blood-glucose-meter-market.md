<p><p>Mobile blood glucose meters are essential devices for diabetics to monitor their blood sugar levels. There are two main types of blood glucose meters on the market: those that use the glucose oxidase method and those that use the glucose dehydrogenase method. The glucose oxidase method measures the amount of glucose in the blood by oxidizing it, while the glucose dehydrogenase method measures glucose by converting it to gluconic acid. Both methods are accurate and widely used by patients to manage their diabetes effectively.</p></p>
