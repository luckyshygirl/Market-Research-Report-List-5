<p><p>Color masterbatch is a concentrated mixture of pigments or additives encapsulated during a heat process into a carrier resin which is then cooled and cut into a granular shape. This granular form allows for easy incorporation into various plastic manufacturing processes to impart color to plastic products. Color masterbatch is widely used in industries such as packaging, consumer goods, automotive, and construction.</p><p>According to market research, the Color Masterbatch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.5% during the forecast period.
