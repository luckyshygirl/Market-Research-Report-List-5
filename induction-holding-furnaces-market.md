<p><ul><li>Coreless Holding Furnaces</li><li>Channel Holding Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Induction holding furnaces are commonly categorized into two main types: coreless holding furnaces and channel holding furnaces. Coreless holding furnaces are known for their efficient and uniform heating capabilities, making them ideal for melting a wide range of metals. On the other hand, channel holding furnaces are designed for holding and maintaining the temperature of molten metal for longer periods of time. Both types of furnaces play a crucial role in foundries and manufacturing industries where precise and controlled heating is required.</p></p>
