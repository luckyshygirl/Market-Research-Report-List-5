<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Mobile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed Solar Traffic Lights are stationary lights that are permanently installed at intersections or roadways. They are powered by solar panels and are connected to the grid for continuous operation. Mobile Solar Traffic Lights are movable units that can be easily transported to different locations where temporary traffic control is needed, such as construction sites or events. They provide the same safety benefits as fixed lights but offer more flexibility in implementation. Both types are growing in popularity due to their energy efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
