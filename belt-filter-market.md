<p><p>Belt filters are categorized into two types based on their orientation: Horizontal Belt Filters and Vertical Belt Filters. Horizontal Belt Filters are commonly used for large-scale sludge dewatering applications due to their high throughput capacity and efficient cake washing capabilities. Vertical Belt Filters, on the other hand, are preferred for applications where space is limited or when a continuous operation is required. Both types of belt filters play a crucial role in the wastewater treatment industry by effectively separating solids from liquids.</p></p>
