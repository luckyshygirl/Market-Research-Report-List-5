<p><p>Small Ubiquitin-Like Modifier (SUMO) proteins play a crucial role in post-translational modification and regulation of various cellular processes. There are four main types of SUMO proteins: SUMO-1, SUMO-2, SUMO-3, and SUMO-4. Each type has specific functions and target proteins within the cell. SUMO-1 is involved in nuclear-cytoplasmic transport, while SUMO-2 and SUMO-3 are essential for stress response and protein quality control. SUMO-4 is less understood but is believed to play a role in immune regulation.</p></p>
