<p><p>Ion Beam Sputtering Equipment market consists of two main types: Single Ion Beam Source and Dual Ion Beam Source. Single Ion Beam Source systems utilize only one ion beam for the sputtering process, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, Dual Ion Beam Source systems use two ion beams for enhanced control and precision in the sputtering process. These systems are preferred for applications that require higher levels of accuracy and control over thin film deposition.</p></p>
