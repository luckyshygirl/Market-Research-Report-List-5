<p><p>Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (ELCB) are safety devices used to protect against electrical shock and fires caused by earth faults in electrical circuits. There are two main types of ELCBs: Voltage Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (VELCB) and Current Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (CELCB). VELCBs detect voltage imbalances between the live and neutral conductors, while CELCBs detect leakage current to ground. Both types of ELCBs automatically disconnect the power supply when they detect a fault, preventing potential hazards.</p></p>
