<p><ul><li>Alkaline Cleaners</li><li>Acid Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal cleaners market can be categorized into two main types: alkaline cleaners and acid cleaners. Alkaline cleaners are typically used to remove oil, grease, and other organic soils from metal surfaces. They are highly effective in cleaning heavy-duty industrial equipment. On the other hand, acid cleaners are used for removing rust, scale, and mineral deposits from metal surfaces. They are commonly used in industries where metal parts are exposed to harsh environmental conditions. Both types of cleaners play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness and longevity of metal surfaces.</p></p>
