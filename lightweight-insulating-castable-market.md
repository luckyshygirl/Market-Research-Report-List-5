<p><p>Lightweight insulating castable market offers two main types: casting type and dry gunning type. Casting type involves pouring the mixture into molds to create custom shapes, making it ideal for complex insulation needs. Dry gunning type, on the other hand, is applied using a high-pressure gunning machine, providing a faster and more efficient option for large surface areas. Both types offer excellent thermal insulation properties, making them popular choices for a variety of industrial applications.</p></p>
