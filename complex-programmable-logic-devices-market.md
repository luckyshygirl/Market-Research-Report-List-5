<p><p>Complex Programmable Logic Devices (CPLDs) are available in two main market types: SMT/SMD CPLD and Through Hole CPLD. SMT/SMD CPLDs are surface-mount devices that are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them suitable for compact and space-constrained applications. On the other hand, Through Hole CPLDs are mounted through holes in the PCB, offering a more traditional and secure connection. Both types of CPLDs have their unique advantages and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements of the design.</p></p>
