<p><p>Silicon Carbide Varistors are electrical devices used for surge protection in various applications. The two main types of Silicon Carbide Varistors are Tube Varistors and Disc Varistors. Tube Varistors are cylindrical in shape and are typically used in high power applications, while Disc Varistors are flat and are commonly used in low to medium power applications. Both types of varistors function by changing resistance with changes in voltage, providing protection against overvoltage conditions to prevent damage to sensitive electronics.</p></p>
