<p><p>In the Office Sound Masking Systems market, there are two main types: Networked and Non-Networked systems. Networked systems use a central control unit to manage sound levels and distribution throughout an office space. This allows for more precise control and customization of the sound masking experience. Non-Networked systems, on the other hand, operate independently without a central control unit. They are typically easier to install and more cost-effective but offer less flexibility in terms of customization and control.</p></p>
