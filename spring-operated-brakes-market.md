<p><p>Spring-operated brakes are a crucial safety feature in various industrial applications. There are two main types of spring-operated brakes - hydraulic and electromagnetic. Hydraulic spring-operated brakes use hydraulic pressure to engage the brake pads, while electromagnetic spring-operated brakes use electromagnetic force to release the brake. Both types are designed to provide reliable braking performance and are suitable for a wide range of applications, such as conveyor systems, cranes, and winches. Each type offers unique advantages based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
