<p><ul><li>100% Low-floor Tram</li><li>70% Low-floor Tram</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low-floor trams are categorized based on the percentage of the tram's floor being low to the ground. A 100% low-floor tram means the entire floor of the tram is low, providing easy accessibility for passengers, including those with mobility issues. On the other hand, a 70% low-floor tram has a majority of its floor low to the ground but may have some areas that are not fully accessible. Both types cater to different market segments, with 100% low-floor trams being more inclusive and 70% low-floor trams offering a balance between accessibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
