<p><p>The closed system transfer device market includes three main types: Closed Vial Access Devices, Closed Syringe Safety Devices, and Closed Bag/Line Access Devices. Closed Vial Access Devices are designed to safely transfer medication from a vial to an administration set without exposure to outside contaminants. Closed Syringe Safety Devices help prevent needle injuries and reduce the risk of needlestick injuries. Closed Bag/Line Access Devices are used to securely transfer medications between IV bags and administration sets to maintain a closed system and reduce the risk of contamination.</p></p>
