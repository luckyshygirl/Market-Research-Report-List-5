<p><p>Battery electrolyte market can be categorized into two main types: liquid electrolyte and gel electrolyte. Liquid electrolytes are widely used in conventional batteries due to their high conductivity and low cost. On the other hand, gel electrolytes are becoming increasingly popular in next-generation batteries for their improved safety and stability. Gel electrolytes have a solid-like consistency, reducing the risk of leakage and enhancing the overall performance of the battery. Both types play a crucial role in determining the efficiency and lifespan of batteries.</p></p>
