<p><p>Gasoline fuel pumps market includes two main types, mechanical fuel pumps and electric fuel pumps. Mechanical fuel pumps operate through the rotation of an engine’s camshaft or crankshaft, which creates a vacuum to draw fuel into the engine. On the other hand, electric fuel pumps are powered by an electric motor and provide a consistent flow of fuel to the engine. Both types of fuel pumps have their own advantages and are commonly used in various automotive applications.</p></p>
