<p><p>Real-wood flooring is made entirely of solid wood and offers a natural, elegant look. Engineered flooring consists of a real wood top layer glued onto multiple layers of plywood, making it more stable and resistant to moisture. Laminated flooring is made of multiple layers of synthetic material topped with a high-resolution image of hardwood, providing a cost-effective and easy-to-install alternative to real wood. Each type of hardwood flooring caters to different preferences and budgets, offering a variety of options for consumers.</p></p>
