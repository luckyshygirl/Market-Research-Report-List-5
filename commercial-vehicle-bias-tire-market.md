<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Bias Tire Market includes two main types: Ordinary Bias Tires and Bias Belted Tires. Ordinary Bias Tires are the traditional type of bias tires with multiple layers of crisscrossed ply cords running at 30-45 degree angles to the direction of travel. On the other hand, Bias Belted Tires have an additional layer of belt material between the plies and the tread, providing better stability and tread life. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different commercial vehicle applications.</p></p>
