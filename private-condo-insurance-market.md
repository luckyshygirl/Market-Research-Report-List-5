<p><p>Private condo insurance market types include bare walls coverage, which covers the physical structure of the condo but requires individual owners to insure their own belongings and upgrades. Single entity coverage protects the condo owners collectively, covering common areas and shared structures. All-in coverage combines both bare walls and single entity coverage, providing comprehensive protection for the entire condo property. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of condo owners, offering varying levels of coverage and financial responsibility.</p></p>
