<p><ul><li>Chemical Antidotes</li><li>Physical Antidotes</li><li>Pharmacological Antidotes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical antidotes are substances that directly counteract the effects of a specific poison by chemically interacting with it. Physical antidotes involve methods such as emesis or dilution to remove or neutralize the poison from the body. Pharmacological antidotes work by blocking the toxic effects of a substance through various mechanisms such as receptor antagonism or enzyme inhibition. Each type of antidote has its own specific applications and effectiveness in treating poisonings or overdoses.</p></p>
