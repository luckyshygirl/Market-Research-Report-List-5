<p><p>UV Light Stabilizers are crucial additives used in various industries to protect materials from the damaging effects of UV radiation. Three common types of UV Light Stabilizers include Benzotriazole, Aniline Oxalate, and Benzophenone. Benzotriazole offers efficient UV absorption and prevents degradation of materials. Aniline Oxalate acts as a radical scavenger and inhibits photochemical reactions. Benzophenone absorbs UV radiation and dissipates it as heat, thereby preventing UV-induced degradation. These UV Light Stabilizers enhance the durability and lifespan of products exposed to sunlight.</p></p>
