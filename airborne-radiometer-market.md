<p><p>Airborne radiometers are used for remote sensing applications including measuring temperature, humidity, and other atmospheric conditions. There are two main types of airborne radiometers: Millimeter Wave Radiometers and Interferometric Correlator Radiometers. Millimeter Wave Radiometers operate in the millimeter wave frequency range, providing high resolution measurements for meteorological and environmental research. Interferometric Correlator Radiometers use interferometric techniques to measure small changes in radiation levels, making them valuable for detecting subtle variations in the atmosphere related to weather patterns.</p></p>
