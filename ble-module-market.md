<p><ul><li>Single-Mode BLE Modules</li><li>Dual-Mode BLE Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-Mode BLE modules are designed for low-power, low-cost applications that only require Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity. They are often used in simple IoT devices like sensors and wearables. On the other hand, Dual-Mode BLE modules support both Bluetooth Low Energy and Bluetooth Classic, making them suitable for a wider range of applications that require compatibility with older Bluetooth devices. Dual-Mode modules are commonly used in devices like smartphones, tablets, and smart home appliances.</p></p>
