<p><p>Anti-Static Epoxy Flooring is categorized into two main types based on their electrical properties: Dissipative Type and Conductive Type. Dissipative Type flooring is designed to safely dissipate static electricity that accumulates on the surface, preventing it from building up and causing damage to electronic equipment. Conductive Type flooring, on the other hand, is intended to actively ground static electricity and provide a pathway for its safe dissipation. Both types of anti-static epoxy flooring play a crucial role in minimizing the risk of damage and ensuring the safety of sensitive environments.</p></p>
