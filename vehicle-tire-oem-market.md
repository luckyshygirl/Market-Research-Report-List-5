<p><p>The Vehicle Tire OEM market can be classified into two main types: Radial Tire and Bias Tire. Radial tires are constructed with cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better traction and fuel efficiency. Bias tires, on the other hand, are made with cords that run diagonally across the tire, offering a stiffer ride but at a lower cost. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences, with radial tires being more popular in modern vehicles due to their performance advantages.</p></p>
