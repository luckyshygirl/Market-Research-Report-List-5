<p><p>Continuous pyrolysis plant market consists of two main types: semi-continuous and fully continuous pyrolysis plants. Semi-continuous pyrolysis plants require operators to feed materials and remove char intermittently, whereas fully continuous pyrolysis plants operate non-stop with automatic feeding and slagging systems. Semi-continuous plants are suitable for small-scale operations, while fully continuous plants are preferred for large-scale industrial applications due to their higher efficiency and automation. Both types offer opportunities in the market for converting waste materials into valuable resources sustainably.</p></p>
