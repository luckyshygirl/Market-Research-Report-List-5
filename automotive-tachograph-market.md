<p><p>Automotive tachographs are devices used to record driving time, speed, and distance traveled by a vehicle. Integrated tachographs are built-in systems within the vehicle that are permanently fixed and record data directly onto the vehicle's system. Portable tachographs, on the other hand, are external devices that can be easily removed and transferred between vehicles. Integrated tachographs are more commonly used in commercial fleets, while portable tachographs are popular among smaller businesses or individual drivers who may not require constant monitoring.</p></p>
