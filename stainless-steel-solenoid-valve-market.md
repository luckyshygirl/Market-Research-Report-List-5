<p><p>Stainless Steel Solenoid Valve market types include Normally Open and Normally Closed valves. Normally Open valves are in their default open state when not energized, allowing flow of fluid through the valve. They close when energized. Normally Closed valves are in their default closed state when not energized, blocking flow of fluid through the valve. They open when energized. Both types serve different purposes and industries based on their functioning when energized or de-energized.</p></p>
