<p><ul><li>PUR</li><li>PVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crane cables are typically categorized into two main types: PUR (polyurethane) and PVC (polyvinyl chloride) cables. PUR cables are known for their flexibility and resistance to abrasion, oil, and weather, making them suitable for outdoor and heavy-duty applications. PVC cables, on the other hand, are more cost-effective and provide good resistance to chemicals and moisture. Both types of cables are commonly used in the crane industry for powering and controlling the movement of cranes.</p></p>
