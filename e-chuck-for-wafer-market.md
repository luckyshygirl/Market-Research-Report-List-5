<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E Chuck for Wafer Market includes two main types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type e-chucks use Coulomb force for electrostatic chucking, allowing for easy wafer handling and low cost. On the other hand, JR Type e-chucks use electrostatic force combined with surface roughness to enhance chucking performance in advanced semiconductor applications. Both types offer unique advantages and are widely used in the semiconductor industry for wafer handling and processing.</p></p>
