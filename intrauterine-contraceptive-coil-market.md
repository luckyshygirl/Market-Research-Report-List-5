<p><p>Intrauterine Contraceptive Coil Market consists of two main types: Hormonal Intrauterine Devices (IUDs) and Copper Intrauterine Devices. Hormonal IUDs release progestin to prevent pregnancy by thickening cervical mucus and suppressing ovulation. Copper IUDs work by releasing copper ions that are toxic to sperm, preventing fertilization. Both types of IUDs are highly effective, long-lasting forms of contraception, with hormonal IUDs providing additional benefits such as lighter periods and reduced menstrual cramps.</p></p>
