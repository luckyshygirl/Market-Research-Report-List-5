<p><p>Safety door switches are categorized into two types: mechanical switches and non-contact switches. Mechanical switches rely on physical contact between the switch and the door to detect its status, while non-contact switches use proximity sensors to sense the presence of the door without physical contact. Both types of switches are crucial in ensuring the safety of industrial machinery by immediately stopping the operation when the door is opened, preventing accidents and injuries.</p></p>
