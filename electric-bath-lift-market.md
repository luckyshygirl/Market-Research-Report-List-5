<p><p>Electric bath lifts come in two main types - bath-side lifts and lifts in bathtubs. Bath-side lifts are placed next to the bathtub and operate by lowering and raising the user into and out of the tub. Lifts in bathtubs are installed directly inside the tub and function in a similar way by lowering and raising the user. Both types of electric bath lifts provide a safe and comfortable bathing experience for individuals with mobility issues.</p></p>
