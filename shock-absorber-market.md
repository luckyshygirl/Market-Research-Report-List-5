<p><strong>The Shock Absorber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Tube Shock Absorbers</li><li>Twin-Tube Shock Absorbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-tube shock absorbers consist of a single tube containing a piston that moves oil back and forth to absorb shock. They are cost-effective and easy to install, making them popular for everyday vehicle use. Twin-tube shock absorbers have an inner tube to control fluid flow and an outer tube to store excess fluid. They provide better performance and durability than single-tube shocks, making them ideal for off-road and high-performance vehicles. Both types play a crucial role in minimizing vibrations and ensuring a smooth ride.</p></p>
