<p><strong>The Current Collector for Light Rail Train (LRT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top-Running</li><li>Bottom-Running</li><li>Side-Running</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Top-running current collectors are mounted on the top of the rail and collect current from an overhead wire. Bottom-running collectors are mounted on the bottom of the rail and collect current from a third rail. Side-running collectors are mounted on the side of the rail and collect current from a side arm. Each type of current collector has its own advantages and is used in different applications depending on factors such as cost, complexity, and infrastructure compatibility.</p></p>
