<p><p>Smart parking meter market includes two main types: Single Space meters and Multi Space meters. Single Space meters are installed at individual parking spots and require users to pay for each space separately. On the other hand, Multi Space meters serve multiple parking spots with a single machine, allowing users to pay for multiple spaces at once. Both types offer convenience and efficiency in managing parking payments, but cater to different needs and preferences of users and parking operators.</p></p>
