<p><p>Conventional Metal Forming Machine Tools can be divided into two main types - Bulk Deformation (BD) and Sheet Metal Working (SMW). Bulk Deformation involves shaping metal into desired forms through processes like forging, extrusion, and drawing. Sheet Metal Working, on the other hand, involves forming thin metal sheets into various shapes using techniques like bending, shearing, and punching. Both types of markets cater to different industries and applications, providing solutions for a wide range of metal forming needs.</p></p>
