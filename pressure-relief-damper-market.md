<p><p>Pressure relief dampers are commonly classified into two types based on their mounting orientation - vertical mount and horizontal mount. Vertical mount pressure relief dampers are installed in a vertical position, allowing for efficient air flow control in applications where space is limited. On the other hand, horizontal mount pressure relief dampers are installed in a horizontal position, making them suitable for applications where airflow needs to be redirected or restricted in a specific direction. Both types of dampers play a crucial role in maintaining optimal air pressure and ventilation in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
