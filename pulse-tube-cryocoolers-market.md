<p><p>Pulse-tube cryocoolers are a type of refrigeration system used to cool materials to extremely low temperatures. They are classified into two main types: single stage and two stage. Single stage cryocoolers use a single pulse-tube to cool materials, while two stage cryocoolers use two pulse-tubes in sequence to achieve lower temperatures. Single stage cryocoolers are simpler and more cost-effective, while two stage cryocoolers are able to achieve colder temperatures and are often used in more demanding applications.</p></p>
