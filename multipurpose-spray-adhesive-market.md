<p><p>Multipurpose Spray Adhesive Market offers three main types: Solvent-Based, Water-Based, and Hot Melt adhesives. Solvent-Based adhesives are durable and provide strong bonds, but emit harmful fumes. Water-Based adhesives are eco-friendly and easy to clean up, but may not be as strong as solvent-based ones. Hot Melt adhesives offer fast bonding and are heat resistant, but may not be suitable for all materials. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, allowing consumers to choose the best adhesive for their specific requirements.</p></p>
