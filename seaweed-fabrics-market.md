<p><p>Seaweed fabrics are usually classified based on the type of seaweed used in their production. The three main types are kelp, kombu, and sargassum. Kelp fabrics are derived from different species of kelp seaweed, known for their soft and lightweight texture. Kombu fabrics come from a specific type of kelp known for its durability and strength. Sargassum fabrics are made from sargassum seaweed, which is known for its natural elasticity and ability to wick moisture. Each type offers unique qualities and benefits in the seaweed fabrics market.</p></p>
