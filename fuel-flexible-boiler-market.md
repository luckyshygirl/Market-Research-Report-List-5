<p><strong>The Fuel Flexible Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Fluidised Bed Combustion (CFBC) boiler</li><li>Atmospheric Fluidised Bed Combustion (AFBC)</li><li>Multi fuel firing boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel Flexible Boiler Market comprises of three main types: Circulating Fluidised Bed Combustion (CFBC) boiler, Atmospheric Fluidised Bed Combustion (AFBC) boiler, and Multi fuel firing boiler. CFBC boilers use a circulating fluidized bed to burn fuel efficiently, while AFBC boilers utilize fluidized bed combustion at atmospheric pressure to generate steam. Multi fuel firing boilers have the ability to burn multiple types of fuels, such as coal, biomass, and oil, providing flexibility in fuel choice and ensuring continuous operation.</p></p>
