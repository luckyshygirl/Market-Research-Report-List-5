<p><p>Rotor locks are devices used to immobilize rotors in industrial machinery to prevent unwanted movement during maintenance or repair. Hydraulic rotor locks utilize fluid pressure to secure the rotor in place, while electric rotor locks use an electric mechanism for locking. Both types of rotor locks offer efficient and secure immobilization of rotors, ensuring worker safety and preventing damage to the machinery. The market for rotor locks is driven by the demand for reliable and easy-to-use solutions for rotor immobilization in various industrial applications.</p></p>
