<p><p>Nondestructive Testing (NDT) Equipment comprises various types of testing methods that do not cause damage to the material being tested. Laser Testing involves using laser beams to detect flaws or inconsistencies in materials. Magnetic Flux Leakage uses magnetic fields to identify defects such as cracks or corrosion. Magnetic Particle Testing uses magnetic particles to reveal surface and near-surface flaws. Ultrasonic Testing uses high-frequency sound waves to inspect the internal structure of materials for defects. Each method offers unique advantages and applications in the NDT Equipment market.</p></p>
