Lignosulfonates are derived from wood pulp and bind dust particles together. Petroleum resins form a protective film over surfaces to prevent dust. Polymer emulsions create a durable coating to suppress dust. Tar and bitumen emulsions are sticky substances that trap dust. Other market types may include organic solutions or environmentally friendly options for dust suppression.</p></p>
