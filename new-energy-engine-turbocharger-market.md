<p><p>A New Energy Engine Turbocharger is a type of forced induction system that compresses the air entering the engine to increase power output. It is commonly used in new energy engine vehicles such as electric vehicles, hybrid vehicles, and fuel cell vehicles to enhance performance and efficiency. These turbochargers work by using the exhaust gases to spin a turbine, which in turn drives a compressor to force more air into the engine, resulting in increased power.</p><p>The New Energy Engine Turbocharger Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
