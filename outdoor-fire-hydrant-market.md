<p><p>Outdoor fire hydrants come in three main types: Ground hydrants are installed above ground and are easily accessible for firefighters. Underground hydrants are installed below ground level and are connected to water mains with a visible outlet above ground. Buried telescopic hydrants are similar to underground hydrants but have a telescopic design that allows them to be easily raised to the surface when needed. Each type serves the same purpose of providing quick access to water for firefighting in outdoor areas.</p></p>
