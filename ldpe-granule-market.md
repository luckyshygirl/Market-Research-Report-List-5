<p><p>LDPE Granule market can be classified into two main types based on the manufacturing process - Autoclave Process and Tubular Process. Autoclave Process involves the use of high pressure and high temperature to polymerize ethylene, resulting in granules with specific characteristics. On the other hand, Tubular Process involves the use of a tubular reactor to polymerize ethylene, producing LDPE granules with different properties. Both processes have their own advantages and are used in various industries depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
