<p><p>The automotive brake systems market consists of various types such as Antilock Braking System (ABS), Traction Control System (TCS), Electronic Stability Control (ESC), and Electronic Brake-Force Distribution (EBD). ABS prevents wheel lock-up during sudden braking, TCS optimizes traction on slippery surfaces, ESC helps maintain vehicle stability during sudden maneuvers, and EBD ensures proper distribution of braking force to all wheels. These systems enhance driving safety by improving control and reducing the risk of accidents.</p></p>
