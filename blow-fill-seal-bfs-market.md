<p><p>Blow-Fill-Seal (BFS) is an advanced technology used for the aseptic packaging of pharmaceuticals, food, and personal care products. This method ensures the integrity of the product and extends its shelf life.</p><p>The BFS market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years due to the increasing demand for sterile packaging solutions in pharmaceutical as well as food and beverage industries.
