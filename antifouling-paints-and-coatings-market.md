<p><p>Antifouling paints and coatings are used to protect marine vessels from organisms that can attach to the hull and slow down the ship. Copper-based coatings release copper ions into the water to prevent fouling. Self-polishing copolymer coatings gradually release antifouling agents as the ship moves through the water. Hybrid coatings combine different technologies for longer-lasting protection. Other types of antifouling coatings may include silicone-based or biocide-free options. Each type of coating offers unique advantages in preventing fouling and preserving the ship's performance.</p></p>
