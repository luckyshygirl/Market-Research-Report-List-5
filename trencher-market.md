<p><p>Wheel trenchers and chain trenchers are two main types of trenchers commonly used in construction and agriculture. </p><p>Wheel trenchers are more commonly used for cutting through hard surfaces like rock or concrete, while chain trenchers are typically used for softer surfaces like soil or asphalt. Both types of trenchers are powered by an engine, but they operate differently - wheel trenchers have a rotating wheel with teeth that cuts into the ground, while chain trenchers have a continuous digging chain that moves along the trench.</p></p>
