<p><p>Vacuum turbo pumps are categorized into three main types: Magnetically Suspended Type, Oil Lubricated Type, and Others. </p><p>Magnetically Suspended Type pumps utilize magnetic bearings to suspend the rotor without any physical contact, ensuring smooth operation and minimal maintenance. </p><p>Oil Lubricated Type pumps use oil for lubrication, which can lead to potential contamination but are often more cost-effective than other types. </p><p>The Others category includes a variety of different types of vacuum turbo pumps with different mechanisms and features.</p></p>
