<p><strong>The Traction Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Propulsion Converter</li><li>Integrated Compact Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traction converters are broadly categorized into two types: Propulsion Converter and Integrated Compact Converter. Propulsion converters are used in electric trains and provide power to the traction motors for propulsion. On the other hand, Integrated Compact Converters combine multiple functions such as rectification, inversion, and filtering in a compact design, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. Both types of converters play a crucial role in the efficient operation of electric traction systems.</p></p>
