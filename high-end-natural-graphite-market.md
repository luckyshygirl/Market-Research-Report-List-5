<p><p>High-end Natural Graphite market consists of three main types- Amorphous Graphite, Flake Graphite, and Crystalline Vein Graphite. Amorphous Graphite is the least crystalline form, used in the production of refractories and steel. Flake Graphite is characterized by its thin, flat flakes and is commonly used in batteries, lubricants, and pencils. Crystalline Vein Graphite is the highest quality and most expensive form, found in veins and used in advanced applications such as in the production of graphene and high-tech devices.</p></p>
