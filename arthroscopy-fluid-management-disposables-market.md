<p><ul><li>Pump Management Disposables</li><li>Gravity Management Disposables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arthroscopy fluid management disposables consist of pump management disposables and gravity management disposables. Pump management disposables typically involve the use of a pump system to control the flow of fluid during arthroscopic procedures. Gravity management disposables, on the other hand, rely on gravity to regulate the fluid flow. Both types of disposables play a crucial role in maintaining a clear surgical field and ensuring optimal visualization during arthroscopic surgeries.</p></p>
