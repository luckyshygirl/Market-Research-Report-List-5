<p><p>Seamless Steel Line Pipe Market Types can be categorized into LSAW Pipes, SSAW Pipes, and Other market segments. LSAW Pipes refer to Longitudinally Submerged Arc Welded pipes, which are made by bending and welding steel plates. SSAW Pipes, also known as Spiral Submerged Arc Welded pipes, are made by spirally bending and welding steel coils. The Other market segment includes various types of seamless steel line pipes that do not fall into the LSAW or SSAW categories, such as ERW (Electric Resistance Welded) pipes.</p></p>
