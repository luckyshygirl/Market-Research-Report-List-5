Mechanical suspension seats use a spring and damper system to absorb vibrations and provide comfort to the operator. On the other hand, air suspension seats use compressed air to adjust the seat height and provide a smoother ride. Both types of seats are designed to improve the comfort and safety of operators working long hours on construction machinery.</p></p>
