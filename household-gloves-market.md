<p><p>Household gloves are commonly divided into two main market types: one-off and reusable. One-off gloves are designed to be used only once, typically made of thinner materials such as latex or vinyl. They are convenient for quick clean-up tasks and are often disposable. Reusable gloves, on the other hand, are more durable and made from materials like rubber or neoprene. They are designed for multiple uses and are ideal for more heavy-duty cleaning tasks around the house.</p></p>
