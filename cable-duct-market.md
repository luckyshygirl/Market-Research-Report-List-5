<p><p>Cable duct market consists of two main types: flexible cable duct and rigid cable duct. Flexible cable ducts are made of materials that allow for bending and flexibility, making them suitable for tight spaces or areas where frequent movement is required. Rigid cable ducts, on the other hand, are made of sturdier materials such as PVC or metal, providing more protection and stability for cables. Both types serve the purpose of organizing and protecting cables in various environments.</p></p>
