<p><p>Medical Styrenic Block Copolymers are categorized into different types based on their chemical composition and properties. Styrene Ethylene Butadiene Styrene (SEBS) copolymers are known for their high elastic properties, making them ideal for medical applications such as medical tubing and medical devices. Styrene Isoprene Butadiene (SIBS) copolymers offer good flexibility and softness, making them suitable for wound care products. Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS) copolymers have excellent mechanical properties and are commonly used in medical tapes and adhesives.</p></p>
