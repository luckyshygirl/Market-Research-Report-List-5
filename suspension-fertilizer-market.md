<p><p>Suspension fertilizers are classified into two main types: compound suspension fertilizer and pure inorganic suspension fertilizer. Compound suspension fertilizers are made up of a mixture of different nutrients, such as nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, while pure inorganic suspension fertilizers contain only inorganic nutrients. Both types of suspension fertilizers are used for plant nutrition and can be applied to the soil or sprayed onto plants. They are known for their high nutrient content and ease of application, making them popular choices for farmers and gardeners.</p></p>
