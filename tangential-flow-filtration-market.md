<p><p>Tangential Flow Filtration (TFF) is a technique used in the biopharmaceutical industry to separate and purify biomolecules such as proteins, viruses, and nucleic acids. TFF works by allowing the solution to flow parallel to the filter membrane, with the feed solution tangentially flowing across the membrane surface. This process helps to maintain a high level of productivity and efficiency by reducing the buildup of particles on the membrane and minimizing fouling.</p><p>The Tangential Flow Filtration Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.3% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for biopharmaceuticals, advancements in filtration technology, and the growing adoption of single-use systems in biomanufacturing.
