<p><p>An I/O Expander Chip is a device that expands the input/output (I/O) capacity of a microcontroller by providing additional digital I/O pins. It is often used in applications where the microcontroller does not have enough pins to interface with all the necessary peripherals and sensors. I/O expander chips can be connected to the microcontroller via various communication protocols such as I2C, SPI, or GPIO.</p><p>The I/O Expander Chip Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period. Overall, the market for I/O expander chips is expected to continue growing as the demand for connected devices and automation systems increases.</p></p>
