<p><p>MEMS based resonators are categorized into two main types based on their design and operating principles: series resonance type and parallel resonance type. Series resonance type resonators have a high quality factor and are ideal for applications requiring high stability and precision, such as frequency references. On the other hand, parallel resonance type resonators are more suitable for applications requiring high energy storage and efficient energy transfer, such as in wireless communication devices. Both types cater to different market needs and offer unique advantages in terms of performance and functionality.</p></p>
