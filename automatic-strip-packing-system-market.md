<p><p>Automatic strip packing systems come in two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic systems are designed to operate without any human intervention, utilizing advanced technology and robotics to package products efficiently and accurately. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems require some level of manual labor, such as loading products into the machine or monitoring the packaging process. Both types are widely used in industries where high-speed and precision packaging is essential, but the choice between the two depends on the specific needs and budget of the company.</p></p>
