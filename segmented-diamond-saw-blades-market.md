There are three main types of segmented diamond saw blades in the market: sintered, welded, and electroplated. Sintered diamond blades are made by mixing diamond particles with metal powder and have a longer lifespan. Welded diamond blades are created by welding diamond segments to the blade core and are more cost-effective. Electroplated diamond blades have a single layer of diamond coating and are suitable for cutting softer materials.</p></p>
