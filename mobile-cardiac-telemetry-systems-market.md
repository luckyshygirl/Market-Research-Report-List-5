<p><p>Mobile Cardiac Telemetry Systems market consists of two types - Single Channel and Multi-Channel systems. Single Channel systems are designed to monitor and transmit one lead of ECG data continuously, providing real-time information on a patient's heart activity. In contrast, Multi-Channel systems are capable of monitoring and transmitting multiple leads of ECG data simultaneously, allowing for more detailed analysis of a patient's cardiac health. Both types of systems play a vital role in remote monitoring and diagnosing cardiac issues.</p></p>
